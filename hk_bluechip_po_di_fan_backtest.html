📖 破底翻 Strategy (Cai Sen Method)

Core Concept: Stock price breaks below a support bottom, then recovers above the neckline. This signals that major players (主力) are absorbing selling pressure and about to push prices higher.

📉 Step 1 — 破底: Price breaks below the recent bottom support level
🔄 Step 2 — 翻回: Price recovers back above the neckline (70th percentile of post-bottom recovery)
📊 Confirmation: Volume surge on recovery (>1.2x avg) strengthens the signal
🎯 Target: Distance from bottom to neckline, projected upward (蔡森 formula)
🛑 Stop Loss: Bottom price × 0.97 (3% below bottom)
⏰ Max Hold: 30 trading days

蔡森金句: "破底翻大都会越过前高"
